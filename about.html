Hello! I’m Hunter,

founder of Form First.

I’m certified through the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM) in Personal Training and Corrective Exercise. I’ve worked as a Biomechanics Specialist for X years and have experience in many modalities of the fitness industry from gyms to physical therapy boutiques. My passion is injury recovery and prevention through sustainable corrective functional movement. I focus on correcting and enhancing posture, quality of movement and increased performance. I found my purpose to help others live a pain-free life after years of recovering from multiple injuries and repetitive surgeries. I discovered through functional training; focusing on correcting my posture with biomechanics, I could enhance my overall quality of movement; and as a result, I improved my quality of life. And I will do the same for you! I’m here to listen to your unique story and help you set specific goals. You can expect to be equipped with the tools you need to break-free of a cycle that’s keeping you stuck. During your session, I will find postural weaknesses, assess muscular limitations and lead you through exercises to increase your overall mobility and performance. You will also receive a regimen of these exercises, designed specifically for you, to do between ours sessions. We will work together to bring your functionality back! I look forward to meeting you and hearing about your restorative or performance goals.
